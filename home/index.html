What we are doing
Ramjet Engine
Rocket engine research is one of our main areas of focus, and we have carried out a multitude of cold flow and ignition tests on rocket engines.
Air-breathing
Engine
We primarily focus on the research of the ramjet/scramjets and rotating detonation engines, and have carried out a significant amount of corresponding experimental and simulation work.
CFD Computational
Methods
We have developed various universal CFD software that can be used for simulation of rocket engines, ramjet engines, and other types of engines.
