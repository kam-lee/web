Research Focus
High-energy-density Fuels

Rocket engine is an important carrier for human space activities and high-speed military weapons. For rocket engine system with fixed propellant type, the way to further improve the specific impulse performance of rocket engine by optimizing the rocket engine system scheme and working parameters is basically close to the limit. Improving the energy characteristics of rocket engine fuel itself has great potential to significantly improve the performance level of rocket engine. Since the beginning of this century, with the maturity and application of nanotechnology, there has been a rapid increase in the research on the addition of solid nanoparticles to traditional liquid hydrocarbon fuels to improve fuel energy characteristics. Most of the research still stays in theoretical calculation and evaluation of single droplet combustion characteristics. The experimental research on combustion performance of hydrocarbon fuel containing nanoparticles in rocket engine ground test platform is very limited.

In view of the research status, the research group systematically carried out the experimental study of the rocket engine containing hydrocarbon fuel nanoparticles.

We study the phenomenon of solid deposition caused by slurry fuel combustion, analyzes the influence law of solid particle type and content on deposition, quantitatively calculates the influence results of different fuels on the throat size of rocket engine, and evaluates the influence law of the change of mass fraction of nano solid particles on fuel combustion characteristics and engine performance.
