Research Focus
Ramjet Engines

The dual mode scramjet (DMSJ) facility in HPL provides variable flow velocities form 0.6 Ma to 2.8 Ma, and flow total temperature from 1000 K to 2500 K by the combination of an oxygen-added hydrogen- fueled heater and a convergent-divergent nozzle. The DMSJ is able to research the injecting, mixing, and combustion characteristics of different kinds of fuel consisting of hydrogen, ethylene, liquid fuel, supercritical-phase kerosene, and metal-blended slurry/gelled fuel. In order to capture the fuel and flame in the supersonic flow field, planar laser-induced fluorescence for hydroxyl (OH-PLIF), LIF/Mie combined photography, tunable diode laser absorption spectrum (TDLAS) , high-speed photography, and on-way pressure measurement have been developed and applied.
