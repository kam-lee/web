Research Focus
Single Droplets Combustion

The engine application performance of fuel depends on its combustion in the engine combustion chamber. At the microscopic level, liquid fuel evaporates and burns in the form of liquid droplets in the engine combustion chamber, and the combustion characteristics of a single droplet can provide basic support for engine tests. High-energy solid particles, such as Al and B, have very high energy characteristics. The slurry or gel fuel made by adding high-energy particles and additives to liquid fuel can obtain higher theoretical volumetric calorific value. The gel fuel has high stability and can maintain a stable state for a long time without particle stratification, which has broad development prospects. Particles and additives affect the heat and mass transfer process of the droplet. The combustion process of the pellet fuel droplet is different from that of traditional pure liquid fuel, and the unique micro-explosion phenomenon will occur during the combustion process. Hypersonic propulsion laboratory mainly carried out the single drop combustion characteristics test of hydrocarbon fuel with particles and single component propellant with particles.

The addition of particles and gelling agent to improve the combustion characteristics of droplets, high boiling point gel agent on the surface of the droplet gel layer formed to promote the droplet micro-explosion, micro-explosion of the droplet before the increase in volume, micro-explosion of the vapour injection and secondary droplet generation and other events to increase the rate of combustion of the droplet; the addition of particles to enhance the radiative heat absorption, shorten the ignition time of the droplet, increasing the rate of combustion. In general, containing particles of fuel droplet combustion characteristics of the law of change is as follows:


•   Micro-explosions occur during the combustion of gel fuel droplets, and the addition of particles leads to a reduction in the yield limit of the gel layer, a decrease in the intensity and an increase in the frequency of micro-explosions.

•   Under different ambient pressures, the ignition delay time of single droplets of gel fuel is higher than that of pure fuel, the addition of Al particles will improve the ignition delay time of gel fuel, so that it is lower than that of pure fuel.

•   At different ambient pressures, the micro-explosion process of combustion of single droplets of gel fuel increases the combustion rate, and an increase in the concentration of particles further increases the combustion rate, but the combustion of the particles leads to a longer overall combustion time.
